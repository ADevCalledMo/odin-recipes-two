  • How to make Spaghetti Bolognaise

Ingridients:

  • 1 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 large brown onion, coarsely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
  • 500g Beef Mince
  • 2 tsp Seasoning
  • 1 medium carrot, peeled, finely chopped
  • 1 celery stick, finely chopped
  • 400g can Diced Tomatoes
  • 2 cups Stock Beef
  • 2 dried bay leaves
  • Basil leaves, to serve
  • Parmesan, to serve

Steps:

  1. Heat the oil in a large heavy-based saucepan over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic. Cook for 2 mins or until the onion starts to soften. Increase heat to high. Add the mince. Cook, stirring with a wooden spoon to break up lumps, for 5 mins or until browned.

  2. Stir in the bolognese seasoning, carrot and celery. Cook, stirring, for 3 mins. Add Diced Tomatoes, Beef Stock and bay leaves. Bring to the boil. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer for 20 mins or until sauce reduces and thickens slightly. Season.

  3. Meanwhile, cook Spaghetti in a saucepan of boiling water following packet directions. Drain.

  4. Divide spaghetti among serving bowls. Spoon over the sauce. Top with basil and sprinkle with parmesan.
